- **present a project clearly**(清楚呈現)

- **highlight impact**(強調影響)

- **presentation**(發表)
  → “This is our final presentation.”
- **reflection**(反思)
  → “We reflected on our experience.”
- **impact**(影響核心)
  → “This project has an impact on…”
- **growth**(成長)
  → “We experienced personal growth.”
- **application**(應用)
  → “We can apply this in…”
- **future vision**(未來展望)
  → “In the future, we hope to…”

  → “This project increased our confidence.”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

- **Presentation Flow**(建議投影片結構)
1️⃣ Problem(問題)
2️⃣ Design(設計)
3️⃣ Implementation(實作)
4️⃣ Impact(影響)
5️⃣ Reflection(反思)

- **Presentation Framework**(這段很重要)
(建議直接放投影片)
A Strong Presentation Should:
- **Have a clear problem**(清楚問題)
- **Show real experience**(真實實作)
- **Explain design logic**(設計邏輯)
- **Demonstrate impact**(影響)
- **Reflect learning**(反思)

- **Peer Feedback Language**(幫學生會回饋)
Positive Feedback
“I like your project because…”
“Your idea is strong because…”

Suggestion
“One suggestion is…”
“You could improve by…”

- **Critical Learning Focus**(這週真正核心)
Students learn:
presentation = communication + structure
impact must be visible
reflection shows depth
