# Week 11 ｜Adolescent Narrative Sharing & Ethical Listening

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務)

> Students will learn to:

share personal narratives(敘事表達) in a safe and controlled way
practice ethical listening(倫理傾聽) with respect and awareness
respond to others’ stories without judgment(非評價回應)
recognize emotional resonance(情緒共鳴) and explain it
use reflective language(反思語言) to respond appropriately

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語)

- **ethical listening**(倫理傾聽)
  → “We practice ethical listening in this session.”
- **empathetic listening**(同理傾聽)
  → “We listen to understand, not to respond.”
- **emotional resonance**(情緒共鳴)
  → “This story created emotional resonance.”
- **vulnerability**(脆弱性)
  → “Sharing requires vulnerability.”
- **safe space**(安全空間)
  → “We create a safe space for sharing.”
- **witnessing**(見證關鍵)
  → “We witness stories without judging.”
non-judgment(不評價)
  → “We respond without judgment.”

- **ESP Listening Response Frames**(這週最核心)
(學生最需要的就是這一段)
1️⃣ Acknowledging(接住對方)
“Thank you for sharing.”
“I hear that…”
“That sounds important to you.”

2️⃣ Reflecting(反映理解)
“What I understood is…”
“It seems that you felt…”
“This experience was meaningful because…”

3️⃣ Expressing Resonance(表達共鳴)
“This touched me because…”
“I can relate to…”
“This reminds me of…”

4️⃣ Staying Non-Judgmental(避免評價)
(這段非常關鍵)
❌ “You should…”
❌ “Why didn’t you…”
✅ “I wonder…”
✅ “That must have been difficult.”

## ESP Output Frames(整合輸出)

“I chose to share…”
“The part that touched me was…”
“This resonated with me because…”
“I learned from this story that…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

Task Flow
- **Share**(分享)
“Share one selected part of your narrative.”
- **Listen**(傾聽)
“Listen without interrupting.”
- **Respond**(回應核心)
“Use one acknowledgment + one reflection.”
- **Reflect**(反思)
“What did you learn from this story?”

Ethical Listening Micro-Skills(你這堂課的真正核心)
Students practice:
- **listening without interrupting**(不打斷)
- **not giving advice**(不急著給建議)
- **not comparing stories**(不比較)
- **staying present**(保持在場)

- **Critical Reflection Focus**(升級)
Students learn:
listening ≠ solving
empathy ≠ advice
sharing ≠ performance

- **Teacher Language Upgrade**(你原本很好,我幫你升級)
你原句:
“We are not evaluating stories—we are witnessing them.”
保留,但加一層專業👇
“We are not evaluating stories—we are witnessing them.
Our role is to understand, not to judge.”

- **Safety Language**(補強成可操作)
“You can stop at any time.”
“You can choose how much to share.”
“You are responsible for your own boundaries.”
