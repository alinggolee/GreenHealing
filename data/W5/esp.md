# Week 5 ｜Solitary Bee Workshop: Understanding & Making

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務焦點)

> Students will learn to:

- **describe differences between solitary bees**(獨居蜂) and social bees(社會性蜂)
- **explain pollination**(授粉) and its importance to ecosystems(生態系統)
- **describe and evaluate a bee hotel design**(蜂旅館設計)
- **apply observation**(觀察) to understand environmental needs(環境需求)

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語+用法)

- **solitary bee**(獨居蜂): a bee that lives alone
  → “A solitary bee builds its own nest.”
- **social bee**(社會性蜂): bees that live in groups
  → “Social bees share work and roles.”
- **pollination**(授粉): helping plants reproduce
  → “Pollination supports food production.”
- **habitat**(棲地): a place where animals live
  → “A bee hotel is a type of habitat.”
- **life cycle**(生命週期): stages of development
  → “All bees share a similar life cycle.”

- **ESP Functional Language**(語言功能句型)
1️⃣ Describing Differences(描述差異)
“The difference is…”
“Unlike social bees, solitary bees…”
“They are different because…”

2️⃣ Explaining Role(解釋功能)
“They help…”
“They play an important role in…”
“This supports…”

3️⃣ Describing Design(描述設計)
“This bee hotel provides…”
“It is designed for…”
“It protects bees from…”

4️⃣ Evaluating Design(評估設計)
“This design is effective because…”
“It may not work because…”
“It can be improved by…”

## ESP Output Frames(學生輸出句型)

“Solitary bees are different from social bees because…”
“They are important because…”
“This bee hotel provides…”
“Our design is good because…”
“We can improve it by…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(完整)

- **Describe**(描述)
“Describe the difference between solitary and social bees.”
- **Explain**(解釋)
“Explain why solitary bees are important.”
- **Evaluate**(評估)
“Evaluate your bee hotel design.”

## PBL Connection(關鍵鋪設)

This activity introduces habitat design(棲地設計) as a real-world problem.
Students begin to think like designers(設計者):
What does a bee need?
How can we support its life?
This prepares students for designing green healing projects(綠療方案設計) for real users(真實使用者) later.
