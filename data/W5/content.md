# Week 5 ｜Solitary Bee Workshop: Understanding & Making

## Learning Goals 學習目標

- By the end of this session, students will be able to:
- 本堂課結束時,學生將能夠:
- understand basic knowledge of solitary bees
- 理解獨居蜂的基本知識
- identify differences between solitary bees and social bees
- 辨識獨居蜂與群居蜂的差異
- explain the role of solitary bees in pollination
- 說明獨居蜂在授粉中的角色
- build a simple solitary bee hotel
- 製作簡單的獨居蜂旅館
- describe key features of a bee hotel
- 描述蜂旅館的設計特徵

## Core Concept 核心概念(升級版🔥)

Solitary bees are not dangerous—they are essential.
獨居蜂不危險,而是關鍵物種。
They connect:
它們連結:
plants(植物)
ecosystems(生態系)
human food systems(人類食物系統)
👉 Caring for bees = caring for life
👉 照顧蜜蜂 = 照顧生命
