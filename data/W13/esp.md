# Week 13 ｜Green Healing Workshop I: Experiential Food Therapy and Practice

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務)

> Students will learn to:

- **describe health needs**(健康需求) using appropriate terminology(專業用語)
- **explain functions of herbal ingredients**(草本功能)
apply food-based intervention(飲食介入) for self-care(自我照護)
- **connect cultural knowledge**(文化知識) with health practices(健康實踐)
reflect on bodily experience(身體感受) in a structured way

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語升級)

- **herbal remedy**(草本療法)
  → “This is a herbal remedy for stress relief.”
- **circulation**(循環)
  → “This drink supports blood circulation.”
- **detoxification**(代謝/排毒)
  → “It helps with detoxification.”
anti-inflammatory(抗發炎)
  → “This ingredient has anti-inflammatory effects.”
- **regulation**(調節核心)
  → “It helps regulate the body.”
- **metabolism**(代謝補強)
  → “This supports fat metabolism.”
- **dosage**(劑量重要)
  → “We need to consider dosage.”

- **ESP Functional Language**(這週核心補強)
1️⃣ Describing Health Needs(描述需求)
“This is helpful for people who experience…”
“This is commonly used for…”

2️⃣ Explaining Function(功能說明)
“This ingredient helps…”
“It supports…”
“It can reduce…”

3️⃣ Connecting Body & Food(身體連結)
“This is suitable for people with…”
“This helps the body to…”

4️⃣ Reflecting on Experience(身體反思)
“I noticed that…”
“My body felt…”
“This changed how I think about…”

## ESP Output Frames(整合輸出)

“This herbal drink is designed for…”
“It helps with…”
“This is useful because…”
“After trying this, I felt…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

Task Flow
- **Describe**(描述)
“What did you make?”
- **Explain**(說明)
“What does it do for the body?”
- **Apply**(應用)
“Who is this for?”
- **Reflect**(反思)
“What did you experience?”

- **Intervention Thinking**(這週最重要補強)
Students begin to understand:
food = not just food
food = intervention(介入)

- **Intervention Frame**(加入這段非常關鍵)
“This drink is designed as a simple intervention for…”
“It aims to support…”
“It can be used in daily self-care.”

- **Critical Awareness**(避免迷思)
Students learn:
natural ≠ always safe
more ≠ better
culture ≠ superstition

- **Teacher Language Upgrade**(你原本很好,我幫你升級)
你原句:
“Green healing is not only about ingredients…”
升級:
“Green healing is not only about ingredients.
It is about understanding the body, culture, and responsible care.”

Safety & Ethics Language(這堂課必加)
“This is for general well-being, not medical treatment.”
“Always consider safety and individual differences.”

## PBL / Course Bridge(關鍵銜接)

This week connects:
self-understanding(Week 10–11)
  → symbolic thinking(Week 12)
  → practical intervention(Week 13)
Students move from:
“understanding”
  → “doing”
  → “applying”
