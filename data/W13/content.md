# Week 13 ｜Green Healing Workshop I: Experiential Food Therapy and Practice


## Learning Goals 學習目標

- By the end of this session, students will be able to:
- 在本節課結束時,學生將能夠:
- understand the basic concepts of food anthropology and medical anthropology in relation to green healing.
- 理解飲食人類學與醫療人類學在綠色療癒中的基本概念。
- identify common health needs (menstrual care, eye care, edema, fat metabolism, stress relief) and their connection to herbal food therapy.
- 辨識常見健康需求(經期調理、眼睛保養、水腫、脂肪代謝、壓力舒緩)及其與草本食療的關聯。
- experience hands-on preparation of green healing drinks and foods.
- 體驗綠色療癒飲品與食物的實作製備。
- apply practical techniques for self-care through dietary practices.
- 運用飲食實踐進行自我照護。

## O — Orientation

The class begins with a short guided discussion:
課程以簡短引導討論開始:
“What do you usually eat when you feel unwell?”
「當你身體不舒服時,通常會吃什麼?」
Students share personal experiences related to:
學生分享與以下相關的經驗:
menstrual discomfort, fatigue, stress, or body imbalance.
經期不適、疲勞、壓力或身體失衡。
Teacher introduces the idea of food as cultural knowledge and healing practice.
教師介紹食物作為文化知識與療癒實踐的概念。
Brief explanation:
簡要說明:
Food Anthropology → food as culture, tradition, and meaning
飲食人類學 → 食物作為文化、傳統與意義
Medical Anthropology → food as healing, body regulation, and care
醫療人類學 → 食物作為療癒、身體調節與照護

## D — Delivery

Introduction to key green healing themes:
介紹綠色療癒核心主題:
Menstrual care (warming and circulation-supporting drinks)
經期調理(溫補與促進循環飲品)
Eye care (cooling and anti-fatigue herbs)
眼睛保養(清涼與抗疲勞草本)
Edema (water regulation)
水腫(體液調節)
Fat metabolism (digestive support)
脂肪代謝(消化支持)
Stress relief (calming herbal combinations)
壓力舒緩(鎮靜草本配方)
Instructor demonstrates selected recipes from the workshop materials:
教師示範教材中的飲品製作:
Herbal drinks using local plants (e.g., betel leaf, roselle, cinnamon)
使用在地植物的草本飲品(如荖葉、洛神、肉桂)
Preparation techniques (boiling, soaking, blending, infusion)
製備技術(煮、浸泡、攪拌、浸泡萃取)
Emphasis on:
強調:
safety and dosage awareness
安全與劑量意識
cultural meaning of ingredients
食材的文化意義
connection between body condition and food choice
身體狀態與食物選擇的連結

## I — Interaction

Students work in small groups to:
學生分組進行:
prepare one green healing drink or food item
製作一項綠色療癒飲品或食物
follow step-by-step procedures from the workshop guide
依照步驟進行操作
observe smell, taste, texture, and body response
觀察氣味、味道、口感與身體反應
Group discussion prompts:
小組討論:
What does this drink taste like?
這個飲品的味道如何?
How does your body feel after tasting it?
飲用後身體有什麼感覺?
Who might benefit from this recipe?
誰適合這個配方?
Students record their experience for later reflection
學生記錄經驗以供後續反思

## R — Recap

Whole-class sharing:
全班分享:
One new thing learned
一個新學到的內容
One surprising experience
一個令人驚訝的經驗
Teacher summarizes:
教師總結:
“Green healing is not only about ingredients, but about understanding the body, culture, and care.”
「綠色療癒不只是食材,而是理解身體、文化與照護。」

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage 專業字彙與用法

Herbal Remedy: a natural treatment using plants for health
草本療法:使用植物進行健康調理的自然療法
Circulation: the movement of blood in the body
循環:血液在體內的流動
Detoxification: removing harmful substances from the body
排毒:移除體內有害物質
Anti-inflammatory: reducing swelling or irritation
抗發炎:減少腫脹或刺激
Regulation: helping the body return to balance
調節:幫助身體恢復平衡
Example 例句:
“This herbal drink helps with body regulation and stress relief.”
「這個草本飲品有助於身體調節與壓力舒緩。」

## ESP Discussion Questions 討論問題

How can food be both cultural and medical at the same time?
食物如何同時具有文化與醫療意義?
Which green healing practice do you find most useful for daily life?
哪一種綠色療癒實踐對你的日常生活最有幫助?
What challenges might people face when preparing herbal remedies?
在準備草本療法時,人們可能會遇到哪些挑戰?

## TEEMI Speaking Practice 口說練習

Describing experience 描述經驗:
“Today, I learned how to prepare a herbal drink for stress relief.”
「今天我學會如何製作一種用於壓力舒緩的草本飲品。」
Explaining function 說明功能:
“This ingredient helps improve circulation and reduce discomfort.”
「這個成分有助於促進循環並減少不適。」
Reflecting 反思:
“I was surprised that natural food can have such strong effects.”
「我很驚訝天然食物可以有這麼強的效果。」
