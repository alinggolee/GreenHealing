## ESP Vocabulary & Analytical Usage(分析用語)

- **empathy map**(同理心地圖)
  → “We use an empathy map to organize understanding.”
- **observation**(觀察): what we see or hear
  → “This is an observation, not a judgment.”
- **assumption**(假設): what we think without proof
  → “This may be an assumption.”
- **interpretation**(詮釋): meaning we give
  → “This is our interpretation.”
- **evidence**(證據): support for ideas
  → “What evidence do you have?”
- **pattern**(模式): repeated idea
  → “We noticed a pattern.”
- **insight**(洞見): deeper understanding
  → “This gives us an insight.”

- **ESP Analytical Language Frames**(這是這週最關鍵)
1️⃣ Describing Observation(客觀描述)
“I observed that…”
“I noticed that…”
“They said…”
“They did…”
(避免評價)

2️⃣ Making Careful Interpretation(保留推論)
“This might mean…”
“This could suggest…”
“It seems that…”
“I am not sure, but I think…”
(降低武斷)

3️⃣ Identifying Assumption(辨識推測)
“This may be an assumption because…”
“We do not have enough evidence to say…”
“This is not directly observed.”

4️⃣ Connecting to Evidence(建立證據)
“This idea comes from…”
“We saw this when…”
“This is based on…”

5️⃣ Identifying Patterns(提升層次)
“We noticed a pattern that…”
“Several groups found that…”
“This appears repeatedly.”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

- **Task Flow**(完整語言歷程)
- **Observe**(觀察)
“Describe one real observation from last week.”
- **Interpret**(詮釋)
“What might this mean?”
- **Challenge**(檢視)
“Is this observation or assumption?”
- **Refine**(修正)
“How can you make it more accurate?”

- **Critical Thinking Focus**(你這堂課真正價值)
Students learn:
observation ≠ interpretation
feeling ≠ evidence
empathy ≠ imagination

- **Ethics Language**(補強你原本那段)
- **Respect**(尊重)
“Do not force others to speak.”
- **Confidentiality**(保密)
“Protect identity using pseudonyms.”
Non-judgment(不評價)
“Describe, do not label.”
