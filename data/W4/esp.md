# Week 4 ｜Green Healing Practice

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務焦點)

> Students will learn to:

- **experience green healing**(綠色療癒) through herbal drinks(草本飲品)
- **describe sensory experience**(感官經驗) and body response(身體反應)
- **explain the function of herbal drinks**(功能) in relation to the body(身體)
- **connect food**(食物), season(季節), and wellbeing(福祉)
- **give simple recommendations**(建議) based on needs(需求)

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語+用法)

- **herbal drink**(草本飲品): a drink made from natural ingredients
  → “This herbal drink supports the body.”
- **ingredient**(成分): what is used to make something
  → “This drink uses natural ingredients.”
- **body response**(身體反應): how the body reacts
  → “My body response is relaxed.”
- **cooling**(降溫): reducing body heat
  → “This drink has a cooling effect.”
- **relief**(舒緩): reducing discomfort
  → “It provides relief from stress.”
- **detox**(排毒): removing unwanted substances
  → “This drink supports detox.”
- **wellbeing**(福祉): healthy body and mind
  → “Food is connected to wellbeing.”

- **ESP Functional Language**(語言功能句型)
1️⃣ Describing Sensory Experience(描述感官)
“It tastes…”
“It smells…”
“It feels…”

2️⃣ Describing Body Response(描述身體反應)
“My body feels…”
“After drinking, I feel…”
“It makes me feel…”

3️⃣ Explaining Function(解釋功能)
“This drink helps…”
“It is useful for…”
“This ingredient supports…”

4️⃣ Giving Recommendation(提出建議)
“I recommend this to…”
“This is suitable for…”
“It is helpful for people who…”

## ESP Output Frames(學生輸出句型)

“It tastes…”
“My body feels…”
“After drinking, I feel…”
“This drink is helpful because…”
“I recommend this to…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(完整)

- **Describe**(描述)
“Describe the taste and sensory experience.”
- **Explain**(解釋)
“Explain how this drink affects the body.”
- **Apply**(應用)
“Recommend this drink to a specific person.”

## PBL Connection(關鍵鋪設)

This week connects experience(體驗) with simple intervention ideas(初步介入概念).
Students begin to think:
What does the body need?
What kind of support can we provide?
This is the first step toward designing green healing activities(綠療活動設計).
