# Week 17 ｜Project Refinement & Creative Production

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務)

> Students will learn to:

revise their project(修正方案) based on real experience
- **translate ideas into visual forms**(轉譯為視覺)
- **communicate a clear message**(清楚傳達訊息)
- **present a project logically**(有結構呈現)
- **highlight key impact**(強調影響力)

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語升級)

- **revision**(修正)
  → “We revised our project based on…”
- **improvement**(改進)
  → “We improved our design by…”
- **visual communication**(視覺溝通)
  → “We communicate our idea visually through…”
- **message**(核心訊息)
  → “The main message is…”
- **impact**(影響關鍵)
  → “This project has an impact on…”
- **audience**(受眾)
  → “Our target audience is…”
- **clarity**(清晰度)
  → “We focus on clarity in our presentation.”

- **ESP Functional Language**(這週最關鍵)
1️⃣ Explaining Revision(修正說明)
“We revised our project because…”
“Based on our experience, we changed…”

2️⃣ Presenting Message(訊息傳達核心)
“The main message of our project is…”
“We want the audience to understand that…”

3️⃣ Describing Visual Design(視覺設計)
“This poster shows…”
“This video explains…”

4️⃣ Highlighting Impact(影響力)
“This project helps participants to…”
“It raises awareness about…”

5️⃣ Reflecting Improvement(改進反思)
“This version is better because…”
“We improved clarity by…”

## ESP Output Frames(整合輸出)

“We improved our design by…”
“Our main message is…”
“This project shows…”
“This helps participants to…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

Task Flow
- **Explain**(說明)
“What did you change?”
- **Present**(呈現)
“What is your message?”
- **Evaluate**(評估)
“Is your message clear?”
- **Give Feedback**(回饋)
“One suggestion is…”

- **Visual Communication Framework**(這段很關鍵)
(建議直接放投影片)
A Good Poster / Video Should:
- **Have a clear message**(清楚主題)
- **Focus on one main idea**(單一重點)
- **Be easy to understand**(易懂)
- **Show impact**(呈現改變)
- **Connect to real experience**(連結實作)

- **Critical Learning Focus**(這週真正核心)
Students learn:
doing ≠ communicating
good idea ≠ clear message
impact depends on how you present
