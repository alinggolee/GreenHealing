# Week 16 ｜Field-Based Green Healing Project Implementation


## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務)

> Students will learn to:

implement a designed program(執行方案) in a real context
facilitate activities(引導活動) with participants
- **observe participant responses**(觀察反應)
reflect on experience(經驗反思) with structure
- **analyze effectiveness**(成效分析)
express learning outcomes(學習收穫) clearly

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語升級)

- **facilitation**(引導核心)
  → “We facilitated the session by…”
- **interaction**(互動)
  → “There was strong interaction between…”
- **engagement**(參與度)
  → “Participants showed high engagement.”
- **response**(反應)
  → “Participants responded positively to…”
- **effectiveness**(有效性)
  → “The activity was effective because…”
- **adjustment**(調整重要)
  → “We adjusted the activity when…”
- **reflection**(反思)
  → “This experience helped us understand…”

- **ESP Functional Language**(這週最關鍵)
1️⃣ Describing Implementation(執行過程)
“We facilitated an activity about…”
“The session included…”

2️⃣ Observing Participants(觀察)
“Participants responded by…”
“They showed interest in…”

3️⃣ Evaluating Effectiveness(評估)
“This activity was effective because…”
“One challenge we faced was…”

4️⃣ Reflecting Learning(反思核心)
“We learned that…”
“This experience helped us realize…”

5️⃣ Suggesting Improvement(改進進階)
“Next time, we would…”
“We could improve this by…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

Task Flow
- **Describe**(描述)
“What did you do?”
- **Observe**(觀察)
“How did participants respond?”
- **Analyze**(分析)
“Was your design effective?”
- **Reflect**(反思)
“What did you learn?”
- **Improve**(改進)
“What would you change next time?”

- **Field Reflection Framework**(這段很重要)
(建議放投影片)
5-Step Reflection Model
- **What we did**(做了什麼)
- **What happened**(發生什麼)
- **What worked**(有效)
- **What challenges**(困難)
- **What we learned**(學到什麼)
(這會讓學生不再只寫流水帳)

- **Critical Learning Focus**(這週真正核心)
Students learn:
design ≠ real-world success
interaction shapes outcome
reflection = professional growth

- **Teacher Language Upgrade**(幫你升級)
你原本很好,我幫你加深👇
“Real learning happens when you interact with people,
not just when you plan activities.”
