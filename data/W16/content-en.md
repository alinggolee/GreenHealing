# Week 16 ｜Field-Based Green Healing Project Implementation

## Learning Goals

- implement the Green Healing Project in a real-world setting.
- interact with adolescent girls through designed activities.
- reflect on teaching, communication, and emotional connection.

- ODIR(One-Paragraph Experiential Design)
- In this session, the whole class visits the partner placement school again. Each group carries out its own Green Healing Project Design with adolescent girls. Students work in groups and take turns facilitating their designed activities. The focus is on real interaction, emotional connection, and practical experience. Through this process, students observe how participants respond, how communication happens, and how their design works in real life. The teacher provides on-site guidance and support when needed, but allows students to take the lead in facilitating the session. After the activity, students begin to reflect on their teaching experience, group cooperation, and the meaning of green healing in practice.
