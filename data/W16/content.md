# Week 16 ｜Field-Based Green Healing Project Implementation


## Learning Goals 學習目標

- implement the Green Healing Project in a real-world setting.
- 在真實場域中實施綠色療癒專題。
- interact with adolescent girls through designed activities.
- 透過設計的活動與青少女進行互動。
- reflect on teaching, communication, and emotional connection.
- 反思教學、溝通與情感連結的經驗。

- ODIR(One-Paragraph Experiential Design)
- ODIR(單段式經驗學習設計)
- In this session, the whole class visits the partner placement school again.
- 在本節課中,全班再次前往合作的實習學校。
- Each group carries out its own Green Healing Project Design with adolescent girls.
- 每一組與青少女進行其設計的綠色療癒專題。
- Students work in groups and take turns facilitating their designed activities.
- 學生以小組合作,輪流帶領自己設計的活動。
- The focus is on real interaction, emotional connection, and practical experience.
- 重點在於真實互動、情感連結與實務經驗。
- Through this process, students observe how participants respond, how communication happens, and how their design works in real life.
- 在此過程中,學生觀察參與者的回應、溝通如何發生,以及其設計在真實情境中的運作方式。
- The teacher provides on-site guidance and support when needed, but allows students to take the lead in facilitating the session.
- 教師在需要時提供現場引導與支持,但讓學生主導整體帶領。
- After the activity, students begin to reflect on their teaching experience, group cooperation, and the meaning of green healing in practice.
- 活動結束後,學生開始反思其教學經驗、小組合作,以及綠色療癒在實踐中的意義。
