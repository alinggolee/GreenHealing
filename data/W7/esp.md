# Week 7 ｜Green Healing USR: First Meeting

- **Encounter**(相遇), Placement(安置), and Connection(連結)

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務焦點)

> Students will learn to:

- **use simple English to interact**(互動) with adolescent participants(青少女)
- **facilitate basic activities**(引導活動) and open conversation(開放對話)
- **describe feelings**(感受), reactions(反應), and connections(連結)
apply the Green Healing Triangle(self–others–environment) in real interaction
- **reflect on first field experience**(場域經驗)

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語+用法)

- **encounter**(相遇): meeting someone for the first time
  → “This is our first encounter.”
- **connection**(連結): building a relationship
  → “We try to build connection with participants.”
- **facilitation**(引導): guiding a group activity
  → “Students practice facilitation skills.”
- **environment**(環境): the surrounding space
  → “The environment affects interaction.”
- **relationship**(關係): how people connect
  → “We explore relationships through dialogue.”
- **independence**(獨立): being able to act alone
  → “Solitary bees represent independence.”

- **ESP Functional Language**(語言功能句型)
1️⃣ Initiating Interaction(開啟互動)
“Hi, I’m…”
“Nice to meet you.”
“Let’s do this together.”

2️⃣ Facilitating Activities(引導活動)
“Let’s watch this.”
“Let’s think about…”
“What do you think?”
“There is no right answer.”

3️⃣ Encouraging Sharing(引導分享)
“You can say anything.”
“Take your time.”
“Would you like to share?”

4️⃣ Expressing Reflection(表達反思)
“I felt…”
“This reminds me of…”
“I learned that…”

## ESP Output Frames(學生輸出句型)

“Today, I felt…”
“I was surprised by…”
“This reminds me of…”
“I think this is important because…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(完整)

- **Initiate**(開啟)
“Introduce yourself and greet participants.”
- **Facilitate**(引導)
“Ask one open question and guide discussion.”
- **Reflect**(反思)
“Share one meaningful moment from today.”

## PBL Connection(關鍵鋪設)

This week is the first real user interaction(真實使用者互動).
Students move from:
- **learning**(學習) → facilitating(引導)
They begin to understand:
- **real people have real feelings**(真實情感)
- **connection is more important than content**(連結重於內容)
This prepares students for:
- **empathy building**(建立同理)
- **future project design**(未來專題設計)
