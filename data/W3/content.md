## Core Concept 核心概念

Solar Terms connect time, nature, and the human body.
節氣連結時間、自然與人體。
Green healing connects:
綠色療癒連結:
season(季節)
body(身體)
self(自我)
Self-awareness begins with seasonal awareness.
自我覺察從季節覺察開始。
