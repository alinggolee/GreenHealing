## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語+用法)

- **solar term**(節氣): a seasonal time point in the year
  → “Each solar term shows changes in nature.”
- **seasonal rhythm**(季節節律): natural pattern across seasons
  → “We are part of a seasonal rhythm.”
- **body response**(身體反應): how the body reacts
  → “The body response changes in different seasons.”
- **lifestyle**(生活方式): daily habits and patterns
  → “Solar terms influence lifestyle.”
- **balance**(平衡): a stable and healthy condition
  → “Balance is important for the body.”
- **reflection**(反思): thinking about meaning
  → “Reflection helps us understand ourselves.”
