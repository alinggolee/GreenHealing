# Week 3 ｜Solar Terms & Self Connection


## Learning Goals

- By the end of this session, students will be able to:
- understand the concept of the 24 Solar Terms
- identify their birthday Solar Term
- explain the relationship between season, body, and self
- use AI to explore personal meaning

## O — Orientation

"Let’s begin with something simple."
"Close your eyes for a moment."
"Think about your birthday."
"What was the weather like?
Was it hot, cold, humid, or windy?"
(Pause)
"Now think deeper."
"How did your body feel in that season?
Did you feel active? sleepy? uncomfortable?"
(Pause)
"Your birthday is not just a date.
It belongs to a seasonal rhythm."
"Today, we will discover your Solar Term identity."

## D — Delivery

"Solar Terms are a traditional way to understand time through nature."
"There are 24 Solar Terms in one year."
"Each one shows changes in:
temperature, plants, and human life."

Solar Terms × Body
"In different seasons, the body reacts differently."
In summer → heat, tiredness, emotional instability
In winter → cold, slow energy, low motivation
"So people did not ask:
‘What do I want?’"
"They asked:
‘What does my body need?’"

Solar Terms × Lifestyle
"Solar Terms guide daily life."
what to eat
when to rest
how to stay balanced
"This is not old knowledge.
This is self-care knowledge."

## I — Interaction


### Activity 1: Find Your Birthday Solar Term

"Take out your phone."
"Search: ‘Solar Term birthday’."
"Find your Solar Term."
"Write it down clearly."
(Pause and walk around)
"If you are not sure, raise your hand."

### Activity 2: AI Dialogue Task

"Now, you will use AI as a learning partner."
"Do not copy everything.
Read, think, and choose."

Students type:
"My birthday is __. What Solar Term is it?"
"What does this Solar Term mean?"
"How does this season affect the body?"
"What lifestyle is recommended?"

### Teacher guidance:

"Read the answers slowly."
"Circle or write down 2–3 ideas you understand."
"Choose ideas that feel connected to you."

### Activity 3: Self Connection Reflection

"Now, stop looking at your screen."
"Look at yourself."
(Pause)
"Do you feel similar to this season?"
"Are you calm like winter?
Active like summer?
Changing like spring?"

## Sentence frames:

"My Solar Term is…"
"This season is…"
"I feel similar because…"
"In my daily life, I often…"

### Activity 4: Pair Sharing → Class Sharing

"Turn to your partner."
"Tell your Solar Term story."
"Speak slowly. Use simple English."

Class sharing:
"My birthday is in __ Solar Term.
This season means…
I connect to it because…"

## R — Recap

"Today, you learned something important."
"You are not separate from nature."
"You are part of a rhythm."
"Next week, we will experience this with our body."
