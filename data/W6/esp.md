# Week 6 ｜Reflection & Meaning: From Bees to Life

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務焦點)

> Students will learn to:

- **describe key characteristics of solitary bees**(獨居蜂特性)
- **explain the relationship between bees and the environment**(環境關係)
- **analyze meaning**(意義分析) from ecological examples(生態例子)
- **connect ecological ideas**(生態概念) to personal life(個人生活)
express reflection(反思) and interpretation(詮釋) clearly

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語+用法)

- **independence**(獨立): the ability to act alone
  → “Solitary bees show independence.”
- **responsibility**(責任): taking care of tasks or others
  → “They take responsibility for their offspring.”
- **ecosystem**(生態系統): a system of living things and environment
  → “Bees are part of an ecosystem.”
- **relationship**(關係): connection between things
  → “There is a strong relationship between bees and plants.”
- **survival**(生存): continuing to live
  → “Pollination supports survival.”

- **ESP Functional Language**(語言功能句型)
1️⃣ Describing Characteristics(描述特性)
“They are…”
“They usually…”
“A key feature is…”

2️⃣ Explaining Relationships(解釋關係)
“There is a relationship between…”
“They depend on…”
“This affects…”

3️⃣ Interpreting Meaning(詮釋意義)
“This shows that…”
“This means…”
“We can learn that…”

4️⃣ Personal Reflection(個人反思)
“I feel that…”
“I am similar to…”
“In my life…”
“I realized that…”

“Solitary bees are…”

“This shows that…”

“In my life…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(完整)

- **Describe**(描述)
“Describe one characteristic of solitary bees.”
- **Explain**(解釋)
“Explain the relationship between bees and nature.”
- **Interpret**(詮釋)
“What can we learn from bees?”
- **Reflect**(反思)
“How does this connect to your life?”

## PBL Connection(關鍵鋪設)

This week moves from observation(觀察) to interpretation(詮釋).
Students begin to ask:
What does this mean?
How does this relate to human life?
This prepares students for the next step:
- **understanding human needs**(理解人的需求)
- **building empathy**(建立同理)
- **designing for others**(為他人設計)
