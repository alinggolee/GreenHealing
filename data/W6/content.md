# Week 6 ｜Reflection & Meaning: From Bees to Life

## Learning Goals 學習目標

- By the end of this session, students will be able to:
- 本堂課結束時,學生將能夠:
- reflect on the characteristics of solitary bees
- 反思獨居蜂的特性
- analyze the relationship between bees and the environment
- 分析蜜蜂與環境的關係
- connect solitary bees to personal life and relationships
- 連結獨居蜂與個人生活與人際關係
- express ideas through discussion and reflection
- 透過討論與反思表達想法

## Core Concept 核心概念

Small life forms can carry deep meaning.
微小生命蘊含深層意義。
Solitary bees show us:
獨居蜂讓我們看見:
independence(獨立)
responsibility(責任)
connection(連結)
Life is not only individual—it is relational.
生命不只是個體,而是關係
