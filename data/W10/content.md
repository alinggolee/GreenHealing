# Week 10 ｜Adolescent Development Narrative: Writing the Self

## Learning Goals 學習目標

- By the end of this session, students will be able to:
- 在本節課結束時,學生將能夠:
- understand strategies for writing an adolescent development narrative.
- 理解撰寫青少年發展敘事的策略。
- reflect on their own development (body, mind, cognition).
- 反思自身發展(身體、心理、認知)。
- structure their experience using timeline or episodic storytelling.
- 運用時間軸或事件敘事來組織自身經驗。
- connect personal experience with developmental needs.
- 將個人經驗與發展需求進行連結。

- Preface (Course Framing & Purpose) 課程前言(框架與目的)
- Before we learn to support others, we must first learn to understand ourselves.
- 在學習支持他人之前,我們必須先學會理解自己。
- In the practice of empathy, it is not enough to only try to understand others.
- 在同理的實踐中,只理解他人是不夠的。
- We also need to look inward.
- 我們也需要向內觀看。
- In this Green Healing course, before transforming into someone who helps others, we must first learn how to “hold” ourselves.
- 在這門綠色療癒課程中,在成為幫助他人的人之前,我們必須先學會如何「接住」自己。
- This is the core intention of your midterm assignment:
- 這就是期中作業的核心目的:
- Adolescent Development Narrative.
- 青少年發展敘事。
- Today, we return to our own adolescence.
- 今天,我們回到自己的青春期。
- By doing this work deeply, we prepare ourselves to better understand and support adolescent girls in the future.
- 透過這樣的深度工作,我們為未來理解與支持青少女做準備。

- Assignment Reminder 作業提醒
- This midterm task has two parts:
- 本期中作業包含兩部分:
- Part 1: Adolescent Development Narrative
- 第一部分:青少年發展敘事
- Reflect on your development during adolescence.
- 反思你在青春期的發展。
- Part 2: Support Reflection
- 第二部分:支持反思
- When you faced difficulties at that time:
- 當你當時面對困難時:
- Who did you most hope would support you?
- 你最希望誰支持你?
- What kind of support did you need?
- 你需要什麼樣的支持?

### Teacher Guidance (Opening Message) 教師開場引導

- “Before we hold others, we must learn to hold ourselves.”
- 「在接住他人之前,我們必須先學會接住自己。」
- “This is not just an assignment—it is preparation for becoming a helper.”
- 「這不只是作業,而是成為助人者的準備。」

## O — Orientation

導入(連結前幾週)
Activity: From Understanding Others to Understanding Self
活動:從理解他人到理解自己
Students reflect:
學生反思:
“Last week, you built a persona based on others.”
「上週,你們根據他人建構人物誌。」
“Today, you will become the ‘subject’ of your own story.”
「今天,你將成為自己故事的主體。」

Reflection Questions 反思問題
What did you learn about empathy from the previous weeks?
你從前幾週學到了什麼同理?
How might empathy apply to yourself?
同理如何應用在自己身上?

### Teacher Guidance 教師引導

“Guide students to shift from ‘observing others’ to ‘understanding self.’”
「引導學生從觀察他人轉向理解自己。」
“Help them realize: self-reflection is also a form of empathy.”
「幫助學生理解:自我反思也是一種同理。」

## D — Delivery

講授(寫作策略)
Topic: How to Write an Adolescent Development Narrative
主題:如何撰寫青少年發展敘事

Key Strategies 核心策略
Reflect on Three Dimensions
反思三個面向
Body (physical changes)
身體(生理變化)
Mind (emotions, relationships)
心理(情緒、人際)
Cognition (thinking, beliefs)
認知(思考、信念)

Choose a Writing Structure
選擇寫作結構
Timeline (year-by-year development)
時間軸(逐年發展)
Episodic storytelling (key moments)
事件式敘事(關鍵時刻)

Reflect on Developmental Needs
反思發展需求
What did you need at that time?
當時你需要什麼?
What support was missing or present?
當時有哪些支持缺失或存在?

### Teacher Guidance(核心教學語句)

“This is not just storytelling—it is reflective analysis.”
「這不只是敘事,而是反思分析。」
“You are not only describing what happened, but also why it mattered.”
「你不只是描述發生什麼,而是說明其意義。」
“Encourage students to move from experience to meaning.”
「引導學生從經驗走向意義。」

## I — Interaction

互動(寫作準備與個別指導)

Task 1: Writing Focus Selection
任務一:選擇書寫焦點
Students decide:
學生決定:
Will I focus on body, mind, or cognition?
我要聚焦身體、心理或認知?
Why do I choose this focus?
為什麼選擇這個焦點?

Guiding Questions 引導問題
Which dimension do you want to focus on? Why?
你要聚焦哪個面向?為什麼?
What moment or period is most meaningful to you?
哪個時刻或階段最有意義?

### Teacher Guidance 教師引導

“Encourage depth over coverage.”
「重視深度勝過廣度。」
“One meaningful story is more powerful than many shallow ones.”
「一個深刻的故事勝過多個表面的故事。」

Task 2: Writing Planning
任務二:寫作規劃
Students outline:
學生規劃:
Structure (timeline or episode)
結構(時間軸或事件)
Key experiences
關鍵經驗
Developmental needs
發展需求
Support reflection (Part 2 integration)
支持反思(整合第二部分)

### Teacher Guidance 教師引導

“Ask students to connect experience with need.”
「引導學生將經驗與需求連結。」
“What did you need at that moment—but did not receive?”
「當時你需要但沒有得到的是什麼?」

Task 3: One-on-One Consultation (Teacher–Student Mentoring)
任務三:一對一討論(師生指導)
Each student meets the teacher individually.
每位學生與教師進行個別討論。

Focus of Consultation 討論重點
Clarity of topic
主題清晰度
Depth of reflection
反思深度
Emotional awareness
情緒覺察
Connection between experience and need
經驗與需求的連結

### Teacher Guidance(關鍵精神)

“Listen before guiding.”
「先傾聽,再引導。」
“Do not correct the story—support the meaning.”
「不要修正故事,而是支持其意義。」
“Help students express what they have not yet found words for.”
「幫助學生說出尚未能表達的感受。」

Suggested Feedback Language 建議回饋語句
“You are describing clearly—can you go deeper into your feelings?”
「你描述得很清楚——可以再深入你的感受嗎?」
“What did you really need at that moment?”
「那一刻你真正需要的是什麼?」
“If someone had supported you, what would that look like?”
「如果有人支持你,那會是什麼樣子?」
“How does this experience shape who you are today?”
「這段經驗如何形塑現在的你?」

## R — Recap

總結(反思與意義建構)

Student Reflection Questions 學生反思問題
Which dimension did you choose (body, mind, cognition)? Why?
你選擇哪個面向?為什麼?
What insights did you gain from this writing experience?
你從寫作中獲得什麼洞見?
How do you feel after reflecting on your adolescence?
回顧青春期後你的感受是什麼?
What kind of support did you most need at that time?
當時你最需要什麼支持?

Teacher Summary 教師總結
“Today, you practiced turning inward with honesty and care.”
「今天你練習誠實且溫柔地向內觀看。」
“You are learning to understand yourself before understanding others.”
「你正在學習先理解自己,再理解他人。」

Final Teacher Message 教師最後訊息
“When you can understand your own story, you become more capable of empathy.”
「當你能理解自己的故事,你就更有能力同理他人。」
“Self-understanding is the foundation of helping others.”
「理解自己是幫助他人的基礎。」
