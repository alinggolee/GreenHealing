# Week 10 ｜Adolescent Development Narrative: Writing the Self

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務)

> Students will learn to:

- **describe personal experience**(個人經驗) in a structured narrative(結構化敘事)
analyze development(發展分析) across body, mind, cognition
- **express needs**(需求) and unmet support(未被滿足的支持)
use reflective language(反思語言) to explain meaning
- **connect past experience to present identity**(身份形成)

## ESP Vocabulary & Reflective Usage(專業反思用語)

- **adolescent development**(青春期發展)
  → “Adolescent development includes body, mind, and cognition.”
- **narrative**(敘事)
  → “This narrative describes my experience.”
- **reflection**(反思)
  → “This reflection helps me understand myself.”
- **timeline**(時間軸)
  → “I organize my story using a timeline.”
- **episodic storytelling**(事件式敘事)
  → “I focus on key moments.”
- **developmental needs**(發展需求)
  → “I had emotional needs at that time.”
- **support**(支持)
  → “I needed support but did not receive it.”
- **identity**(自我認同補強)
  → “This experience shaped my identity.”

- **ESP Reflective Language Frames**(這週最核心)
1️⃣ Describing Experience(經驗描述)
“During my adolescence, I experienced…”
“One important moment was…”

2️⃣ Expressing Feelings(情緒表達)
“I felt…”
“I was confused / stressed / alone…”

3️⃣ Identifying Needs(需求辨識)
“At that time, I needed…”
“What I really needed was…”

4️⃣ Reflecting on Support(支持分析)
“I did not receive enough…”
“I wish someone had…”
“Support could have been…”

5️⃣ Meaning-Making(意義建構最重要)
“This experience is important because…”
“This helped me understand…”
“This shaped who I am today…”

- **ESP Analytical Frames**(讓反思變專業)
“I experienced…, which affected my…”
“This shows that my developmental need was…”
“This situation influenced my thinking about…”

## ESP Output Frames(整合輸出)

“My narrative focuses on…”
“One key moment in my adolescence was…”
“At that time, I needed…”
“This experience shaped…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

- **Describe**(描述)
“Share one key adolescent experience.”
- **Reflect**(反思)
“Explain why it is important.”
- **Analyze**(分析)
“What need did this reveal?”
- **Connect**(連結)
“How does this affect you today?”

- **Critical Reflection Focus**(這堂課真正核心)
Students learn:
experience → reflection → meaning
feeling → need → support
past → identity → future helping

- **Emotional Safety Language**(你這堂課必須要有)
You原本有精神,但我幫你轉成可教語言👇
“You can choose what to share.”
“You are in control of your story.”
“There is no need to compare your experience with others.”

- **Teacher Language Upgrade**(你原本很好,我幫你變更精準)
你原句:
“This is not just storytelling—it is reflective analysis.”
升級:
“This is not only storytelling.
It is reflective and analytical work.”

你原句:
“Help students express what they have not yet found words for.”
升級(超強):
“Help students find language for experiences they have not yet expressed.”

## PBL / Course Logic Bridge(關鍵銜接)

This week connects:
- **persona**(understanding others)
  → self-narrative(understanding self)
  → intervention design(supporting others)
Students move from:
“I understand others”
  → “I understand myself”
  → “I can support others”
