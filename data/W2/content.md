# Week 2 ｜Seasonal Awareness: Solar Terms and Green Healing


## Learning Goals 學習目標

> By the end of this week, students will be able to:

- 本週結束時,學生將能夠:
- understand the idea of solar terms and seasonal change
- 理解節氣與季節變化概念
- describe how seasons influence body, mood, and daily life
- 描述季節如何影響身體、情緒與生活
- explain the connection between seasonal rhythm and wellbeing
- 說明季節節律與福祉的關係
- observe and reflect on personal seasonal experience
- 觀察並反思個人的季節經驗
- think about using seasonal elements in green healing
- 思考如何將季節元素應用於綠色療癒

## Core Concept 核心概念

Solar terms help us understand natural patterns across the year.
節氣幫助我們理解一年中的自然變化模式。
Green healing connects:
綠色療癒連結:
environment(環境)
body(身體)
emotion(情緒)
Healing is seasonal and flexible.
療癒是隨季節變動且具有彈性的。
