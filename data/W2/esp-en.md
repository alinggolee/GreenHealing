# Week 2 ｜Seasonal Awareness: Solar Terms and Green Healing

## ESP

## Key Vocabulary

1. solar terms
Traditional system to describe seasonal changes throughout the year.
2. seasonal rhythm
The natural pattern of change across seasons.
3. observation
Carefully noticing details in the environment or experience.
4. adjustment
Changing behavior to fit different conditions.
5. balance
A stable and healthy state between different needs.
6. body awareness
Understanding signals from the body.
7. environmental change
Changes in weather, temperature, light, or natural surroundings.
8. wellbeing
A healthy state of body and mind.

## Sentence Frames
