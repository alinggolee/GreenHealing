# Week 2 ｜Seasonal Awareness: Solar Terms and Green Healing

Seasonal Awareness & Green Healing
## ESP Learning Focus

> Students will learn to:

- **describe seasonal change**(季節變化)
- **explain how seasons affect body**(身體) and mood(情緒)
- **identify seasonal needs**(需求) and adjustment(調整)
- **connect seasonal awareness**(季節覺察) with green healing(綠療)

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語+用法)

- **solar terms**(節氣): a system to describe seasonal change
  → “Solar terms show changes in nature.”
- **seasonal rhythm**(季節節律): natural pattern across seasons
  → “Our body follows a seasonal rhythm.”
- **observation**(觀察): noticing details carefully
  → “Observation is the first step in learning.”
- **adjustment**(調整): changing behavior to fit conditions
  → “We make adjustments based on the season.”
- **balance**(平衡): a stable and healthy state
  → “Balance is important for wellbeing.”
- **body awareness**(身體覺察): understanding body signals
  → “Body awareness helps us make better choices.”
- **environmental change**(環境變化): changes in nature
  → “Environmental change affects our mood.”
- **wellbeing**(福祉): healthy body and mind
  → “Wellbeing is connected to environment.”

## ESP Language Progression

### Level 1: Describing(描述)

“In this season, I notice…”
“The weather becomes…”
“My body feels…”
### Level 2: Explaining(解釋)

“This affects…”
“This influences…”
“This can lead to…”
### Level 3: Applying(應用)

“I adjust by…”
“People need…”
“A suitable activity is…”

## ESP Output Frames

“In this season, I feel… because…”
“My body changes because…”
“I adjust by…”
“A good activity should include…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice

Describe: What do you notice in this season?
Explain: How does the season affect your body?
Apply: Suggest one activity for this season

## PBL Connection

Seasonal awareness(季節覺察) is the first step of program design(方案設計).
Before designing activities, students need to understand:
- **the environment**(環境)
- **the timing**(時機)
- **the needs**(需求)
This week helps students begin to think:
What do people need in this season?
How can we design a suitable activity?
