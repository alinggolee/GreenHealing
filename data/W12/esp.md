# Week 12 ｜Visualizing Life Stories: From Narrative to Creative Expression

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務)

> Students will learn to:

- **transform narrative**(敘事轉化) into visual representation(視覺表現)
explain symbolic meaning(象徵意義) clearly
- **use metaphor**(隱喻) to express emotions(情緒)
- **interpret visual work**(作品詮釋) with empathy(同理)
evaluate creative work(創作評析) without judgment

## ESP Vocabulary & Creative-Analytical Usage(創作+分析用語)

- **visual narrative**(視覺敘事)
  → “This is my visual narrative.”
- **creative expression**(創作表達)
  → “This is a form of creative expression.”
- **abstraction**(抽象化)
  → “I simplified my experience through abstraction.”
- **symbolization**(象徵化)
  → “This image is a symbol of my experience.”
- **metaphor**(隱喻核心)
  → “This is a metaphor for my life.”
- **natural metaphor**(自然隱喻)
  → “I used a natural metaphor.”
- **representation**(再現)
  → “This represents my story.”
- **interpretation**(詮釋)
  → “This is my interpretation.”
- **reflection**(反思)
  → “This reflects my experience.”

- **ESP Creative Language Frames**(這週核心)
1️⃣ Explaining Symbol(說明象徵)
“I chose this symbol because…”
“This image represents…”
“This is a metaphor for…”

2️⃣ Connecting Story to Image(敘事轉換)
“This image comes from…”
“This part of my story is shown by…”

3️⃣ Expressing Meaning(意義建構)
“This reflects my experience because…”
“This shows how I felt when…”

4️⃣ Interpreting Others’ Work(作品詮釋)
“I think this symbol represents…”
“This image makes me feel…”
“This suggests that…”

5️⃣ Giving Respectful Feedback(非評價回饋)
“I appreciate this work because…”
“I noticed…”
“This is meaningful because…”

## ESP Output Frames(整合輸出)

“My visual narrative represents…”
“I chose this symbol because…”
“This image shows…”
“This metaphor is connected to my experience because…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

Task Flow
- **Present**(呈現)
“Explain your visual narrative.”
- **Explain**(說明)
“What does your symbol mean?”
- **Interpret**(詮釋)
“Respond to another student’s work.”
- **Reflect**(反思)
“What did you learn from this process?”

- **Creative Thinking Focus**(這週真正核心)
Students learn:
experience → symbol → meaning
story → image → interpretation

- **Critical Awareness**(避免常見問題)
Students learn:
symbol ≠ decoration
creativity ≠ randomness
interpretation ≠ judgment

- **Teacher Language Upgrade**(你原本很好,我幫你升級)
你原句:
“This is not about artistic skill, but about expression.”
升級:
“This is not about artistic skill.
It is about meaningful expression.”

你原句:
“Why this image? What does it represent?”
升級:
“Why did you choose this image?
What does it represent in your story?”
