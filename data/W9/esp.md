# Week 9 ｜Persona: Refining Understanding through Interview

- **Persona**(人物誌): Refining Understanding(深化理解) through Interview(訪談)

## ESP Learning Focus(語言任務)

> Students will learn to:

- **describe a persona**(人物誌) based on evidence(證據)
- **ask effective interview questions**(訪談提問)
use probing questions(追問) to deepen understanding
revise ideas(修正想法) based on new information
explain changes(解釋修正) clearly and logically

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語)

- **persona**(人物誌)
  → “Our persona represents a real user.”
- **representation**(再現)
  → “This is a representation, not a real person.”
- **evidence**(證據)
  → “This is based on evidence.”
- **assumption**(假設)
  → “This may be an assumption.”
- **interview**(訪談)
  → “We conducted an interview.”
- **insight**(洞見)
  → “We gained a new insight.”
- **revision**(修正)
  → “We revised our persona.”

- **ESP Functional Language**(核心補強)
1️⃣ Describing Persona(描述人物)
“Our persona is a 15-year-old student who…”
“She often…”
“She feels…”
“She struggles with…”

2️⃣ Justifying with Evidence(證據支撐)
“This is based on…”
“We observed that…”
“This comes from our empathy map.”

3️⃣ Interview Questioning(訪談提問最重要)
- **Open Questions**(開放式)
“Can you tell us about…”
“How do you feel when…”
“What usually happens when…”

- **Probing Questions**(追問)
“Can you give an example?”
“Why do you think that happens?”
“What do you mean by that?”

4️⃣ Revising Understanding(修正)
“Before the interview, we thought…”
“After the interview, we realized…”
“This changed our understanding because…”

5️⃣ Recognizing Assumptions(辨識偏差)
“We assumed that…, but…”
“This was not accurate because…”
“We misunderstood…”

## ESP Output Frames(整合輸出)

“Our persona represents…”
“This is based on…”
“Before the interview, we thought…”
“After the interview, we changed…”
“This insight is important because…”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice(升級版)

Task Flow
- **Present**(呈現)
“Introduce your persona.”
- **Justify**(說明)
“Explain the evidence.”
- **Interview**(訪談)
“Ask one open and one probing question.”
- **Revise**(修正)
“Explain what changed and why.”

- **Interview Design Support**(這段是你原本沒有但超關鍵)
Good Question vs Weak Question
❌ “Do you like school?”
✅ “What is one thing you like or dislike about school, and why?”
❌ “Are you lonely?”
✅ “When do you feel most alone?”

- **Teacher Guidance**(訪談倫理+品質)
“Do not ask leading questions.”
(避免引導)
“Do not try to confirm your idea.”
(避免驗證偏見)
“Try to discover something new.”
(強調探索)

- **Critical Thinking Focus**(這週核心)
Students learn:
persona ≠ stereotype
interview ≠ confirmation
understanding = revision

Ethics & Professional Language(補強)
- **Respect**(尊重)
“Listen without interrupting.”
Non-judgment(不評價)
“Do not label or judge.”
- **Accuracy**(準確)
“Do not over-interpret.”
