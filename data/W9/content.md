# Week 9 ｜Persona: Refining Understanding through Interview


## Learning Goals 學習目標

- By the end of this session, students will be able to:
- 本堂課結束時,學生將能夠:
- transform empathy maps into personas
- 將同理心地圖轉化為人物誌
- construct a realistic representation of an adolescent girl
- 建構具真實性的青少女人物形象
- refine understanding through professional interview
- 透過專業訪談深化理解
- recognize and revise assumptions
- 辨識並修正既有推測

## Core Concept 核心概念

A persona is not a guess.
人物誌不是猜測
It is evidence-based understanding.
而是基於證據的理解
Understanding must be revised.
理解需要被修正

## D — Delivery

內容講授(概念釐清)

What is a Persona? 什麼是人物誌?
A representative individual
一個具代表性的個體
Based on real observations
建立在真實觀察之上
Used to guide design decisions
用於引導設計決策

### Teacher Guidance 教師引導

“Remind students:
A persona must come from evidence—not imagination.”
「提醒學生:人物誌必須來自證據,而非想像。」

## I — Interaction

互動(人物誌建構)

Task 1: Create a Persona (30 minutes)
任務一:建立人物誌(30分鐘)
Students work in groups.
學生分組進行。

Persona Content 人物誌內容
Name (pseudonym)
姓名(化名)
Personality
個性
Daily life
日常生活
Goals
目標
Pain points
困境/痛點
Needs
需求

### Teacher Guidance 教師引導

“Ask: What data supports this?”
「提問:這個內容有什麼資料支持?」
“Is this based on observation or assumption?”
「這是來自觀察還是假設?」

Task 2: Interview Preparation
任務二:訪談準備
Each group prepares questions.
每組準備訪談問題。

Instruction 指示
Students present:
學生需說明:
What they want to ask
他們想問什麼
Why they want to ask
為什麼這樣問
Content:
(略)

### Teacher Guidance 教師引導

“Guide students to ask open-ended questions.”
「引導學生提出開放式問題。」
“Encourage curiosity, not confirmation.”
「鼓勵好奇,而非驗證既有想法。」

Task 3: Interview with Institutional Teacher
任務三:與機構教師進行訪談
Guest: Teacher from placement school
來賓:合作學校教師
Students conduct interview to clarify their personas.
學生透過訪談釐清人物誌內容。

Teacher Role (facilitator) 教師角色(引導者)
Ensure respectful interaction
確保互動尊重
Support deeper questioning
支持更深入提問
Redirect if students become judgmental
若學生出現評斷,協助重新導向

Task 4: Revise Persona
任務四:修正人物誌
Students update their persona after the interview.
學生於訪談後修正人物誌。

Reflection Questions 反思問題
What changed?
有什麼改變?
What did you misunderstand before?
你之前誤解了什麼?
What new insights did you gain?
你獲得了哪些新的理解?

### Teacher Guidance 教師引導

“Highlight how understanding evolves.”
「強調理解是會演變的。」
“Encourage students to accept uncertainty.”
「鼓勵學生接受不確定性。」

## R — Recap

總結(教師整合)

Group Sharing 小組分享
What did you learn from the persona?
你從人物誌中學到了什麼?
What is empathy now?
現在你如何理解同理?

訪談如何改變你的想法?

Teacher Summary 教師總結
“Empathy is not a one-time activity.”
「同理不是一次性的活動。」
“It requires continuous revision.”
「它需要持續修正。」

Final Teacher Message 教師最後訊息
“You cannot fully understand another person.”
「你無法完全理解另一個人。」
“But you can become more careful, respectful, and aware.”
「但你可以變得更謹慎、更尊重、更有覺察。」
