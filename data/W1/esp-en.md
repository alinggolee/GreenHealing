# Week 1 ｜Course Introduction: What Is Green Healing?

## ESP

## Key Vocabulary

1. green healing
Using natural elements and environmental experience to support wellbeing.
2. wellbeing
A healthy and balanced state in body, mind, and daily life.
3. reflection
Thinking carefully about an experience and what it means.
4. field practice
Applying what we learn in a real setting.
5. program design
Planning activities with a clear purpose, process, and outcome.
6. self-care
Ways of taking care of one’s physical and emotional needs.
7. engagement
Active participation and meaningful involvement.
8. environment
The natural and social surroundings in which people live and learn.
