# Week 1 ｜Course Introduction: What Is Green Healing?


## Learning Goals 學習目標

> By the end of this week, students will be able to:

- 本週結束時,學生將能夠:
- describe the basic idea of green healing in simple English.
- 用簡單英文描述「綠色療癒」的基本概念
- explain why this course connects nature, wellbeing, and helping relationships.
- 說明本課程如何連結自然、福祉與助人關係
- understand the course structure (experience, reflection, program design, field practice).
- 理解課程架構(經驗、反思、方案設計、場域實作)
- identify connections to SDG15, self-care, and social engagement.
- 辨識與SDG15、自我照顧與社會參與的關聯
- reflect on their own relationship with the environment.
- 反思自己與環境的關係

## Core Idea 核心概念

Green healing is not only about nature activities.
綠色療癒不只是自然活動。
It is about relationships between:
它關注的是三種關係:
self(自我)
others(他人)
environment(環境)
Learning process:
學習歷程:
experience → reflection → design → action → reflection
經驗 → 反思 → 設計 → 行動 → 再反思
