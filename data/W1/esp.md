# Week 1 ｜Course Introduction: What Is Green Healing?

## ESP Learning Focus

> Students will learn to:

- **describe the concept of green healing**(綠色療癒)
- **explain relationships between nature**(自然), wellbeing(福祉), and helping relationships(助人關係)
- **understand the course structure**(課程架構)
- **reflect on personal connection with the environment**(環境連結)

## ESP Vocabulary & Professional Usage(專業用語+用法)

- **green healing**(綠色療癒): using nature to support wellbeing
  → “Green healing supports both body and mind.”
- **wellbeing**(福祉): a healthy and balanced state
  → “Wellbeing includes physical and emotional health.”
- **reflection**(反思): thinking about experience and meaning
  → “Reflection helps us understand our experience.”
- **program design**(方案設計): planning activities with purpose
  → “Program design connects ideas with action.”
- **field practice**(場域實作): applying learning in real settings
  → “Students learn through field practice.”
self-care(自我照護): caring for one’s own needs
  → “Self-care is part of professional practice.”
- **engagement**(參與): active involvement
  → “Learning requires active engagement.”
- **environment**(環境): natural and social surroundings
  → “The environment influences our wellbeing.”

## TEEMI Speaking Practice

Describe: What is green healing?
Explain: Why is nature important for wellbeing?
Reflect: How does nature support your daily life?

## PBL Connection

In this course, students will not only learn ideas, but also design real activities(設計真實活動).
Green healing is not only personal(個人), but also relational(關係) and social(社會).
This week builds the foundation for future project design(專題設計) by asking:
What does healing mean?
How can we support others through nature?
